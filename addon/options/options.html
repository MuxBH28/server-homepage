Extension Features Explained

This integration brings automation utilities out of the local app dashboard directly into your regular browsing sessions:

Dynamic Badge Display

The system badge text layer in your toolbar dynamically mirrors your server's selected hardware metric stream metrics.

Smart Color Alerts

The runtime engine tracks health parameters. Badge backgrounds shift matching load states (Green: Normal, Orange: Warning, Red: Error).

Context Menu Dispatch

Right-click elements inside any tab window to trigger quick link dispatches straight into your operational sidebar array.

Automated Heartbeats

The persistent background background routine schedules asynchronous intervals evaluating infrastructure reachability matrix.
